About

Felicia Schwemmer, MSc

I'm a registered Counselling Psychologist with a Master of Science in Psychology (University of Konstanz) and 6 years of experience in psychological counselling.

My work is rooted in Cognitive-Behavioural Therapy (CBT), adapted with an integrative and needs-oriented lens. I believe every person's path is unique — so I take the time to understand your situation deeply before co-creating strategies that truly fit your life.

Before focusing on private practice, I worked as a clinical psychologist in trauma diagnostics for a refugee project, as a psychotherapist-in-training in psychiatry, and currently hold a position as a school psychologist — experiences that have shaped my warm, grounded and practical approach.

My Approach

Evidence-based,
human-centred care

Cognitive-Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

The backbone of my work. CBT helps identify unhelpful thought patterns and behaviours, replacing them with more adaptive ways of thinking and acting — with lasting, measurable results.

Needs-Oriented & Integrative

No two people are the same. I adapt my methods to suit your unique goals, personality, and pace — drawing from a wide therapeutic toolkit to find what works best for you.

Trauma-Informed Practice

Informed by years of clinical experience in trauma diagnostics and psychiatry, I create a safe, non-judgmental space where difficult experiences can be explored at your own pace.

Relationship First

Therapeutic change begins with trust. I prioritise building a genuine, collaborative relationship so that you feel truly understood before we work on strategies and solutions together.
